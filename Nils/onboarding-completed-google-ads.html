This is the most important step. Your Google Business Profile allows your ads to show your location, phone number, and reviews. Without it, we cannot run the full campaign. It is free and takes less than 5 minutes to create.

📈 Why this matters for your Google Ads

  • Your ads will show your address, phone number, and star ratings — which increases clicks
  • It verifies your business to Google, which improves your ad quality score and lowers your cost per click
  • Without it, we cannot attach location extensions to your campaign
  • Businesses with a verified profile get significantly better ad performance
