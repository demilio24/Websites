What We Offer

Lessons Built Around Your Goals

Whether your child is just starting out or you are an adult ready to finally learn, we have a program that fits.

Children's Swim Lessons

Private one-on-one lessons for kids ages 3 and up. We build from water safety to stroke development at your child's own pace.

Our children's program follows a proven progression curriculum refined over 11 years and hundreds of students. Every lesson is completely private. 20 to 30 minutes of focused, one-on-one time in the water.

We start with water safety and buoyancy before introducing any strokes. This allows children to feel safe and in control before any complex movement is introduced. Whether your child is terrified of putting their face underwater or preparing for a swim team tryout, the program meets them exactly where they are.

Parents receive daily feedback and customized at-home practice recommendations after every session.

Ages: 3 years and up
Length: 20–30 minutes
Format: Private only
Locations: Community & in-home pools, San Diego County

Adult Swim Lessons

Whether you've never swum before or want to improve your technique, our 8-week adult progression program will get you confident in the water.

It is never too late to learn. Our adult program is built specifically for people who feel anxious in the water, have never learned to swim properly, or want to refine their technique in a comfortable, judgment-free environment.

Safe Space Swim uses a unique 8-week progression system for adults that starts exactly where you are. No rushing. No group pressure. Just one instructor, one student, and a clear path forward.

This is also one of the most important things you can do for your family. A parent who can swim is a parent who can save their child.

Ages: 16 years and up
Length: 20–30 minutes
Format: Completely private
Program: 8-week progression

Private Lifeguarding

Pool parties in San Diego are a way of life. But having 20 kids near water is a serious responsibility. Our certified lifeguard service lets you relax and enjoy your event while a trained professional watches the water.
