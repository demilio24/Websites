🖼️ Image Revisions
🚨 READ THIS BEFORE YOU TOUCH ANYTHING BELOW
  1. Open your website and find the exact image you want replaced.
  2. Right-click directly on that image.
  3. A menu will pop up. Click "Copy Image Address" (sometimes called "Copy Image Link").
  4. Paste that copied link into the "Current Image URL" field below.
  5. Upload your new image somewhere (Google Drive, Dropbox, your own site) and paste its full URL into the replacement field.
☝️ DO NOT write "the logo," "the banner," or "the photo on the homepage." We have no idea which image you mean. You MUST paste the actual URL from right-clicking the image. No URL = your request gets rejected. It takes 5 seconds. Do it.
🎬 Video Changes
🚨 READ THIS BEFORE YOU TOUCH ANYTHING BELOW
  1. Open your website and find the exact video you want replaced.
  2. Right-click directly on the video player.
  3. A menu will pop up. Click "Copy Video Address" (sometimes called "Copy Video Link").
  4. Paste that copied link into the "Current Video URL" field below.
  5. For the new video, paste the full URL (YouTube link, Vimeo link, or direct video URL) into the replacement field.
☝️ DO NOT write "the intro video" or "the video on page 2." We need the actual URL. Right-click the video. Copy the address. Paste it. That is all. No URL = request rejected. This is not optional.
